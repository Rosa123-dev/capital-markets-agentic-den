What is the Capital Markets Agenda?

A practitioner-first AI learning journey built specifically for Capital Markets professionals.

The Capital Markets Agenda takes individuals and teams from foundational AI awareness through to hands-on agentic workflows — across two leading platforms, with a focus on real Capital Markets use cases. Each session builds on the last, culminating in Agent Den labs where participants design and deploy AI agents for their day-to-day work.

01

Build AI Fluency

Give every capital markets professional hands-on familiarity with the AI tools reshaping the industry.

02

Real Workflows, Real Impact

Every session is anchored to Capital Markets use cases — research, analysis, client delivery, and beyond.

03

Deploy AI Agents

Each programme culminates in an Agent Den — where you build and deploy a real AI agent for your work.

04

Two Platforms, One Journey

Master Microsoft Copilot and Anthropic Claude — the two most relevant AI platforms in enterprise today.

05

Practice-Wide Visibility

A shared agent tracker gives leadership real-time visibility of every agent built across the programme.

06

One Session Mandatory

Sessions run at lunchtime and after-work so there's a slot for everyone. We ask that at least one is made mandatory for the team.

Where Are We Today?

Most people in capital markets are aware of AI — but very few are actually using it in their day-to-day work. This programme is designed to move everyone along the journey, from knowing what AI is to genuinely embedding it into how we work.

Group 1 • Aware

Knows the terminology. Has never built an agent.

Group 2 • Capable

Has built one — but isn't using agents day to day.

Group 3 • Embedded

Regularly builds, ideates, and shares with the team.

Goal: everyone reaches Group 3

How Each Workshop Runs

1

Learn & Explore

We frame a real problem together and think through where AI could help. No technical knowledge needed — just curiosity.

2

Build Something

The hands-on part. You actually build an AI agent — either from the session or something from your own work. Support is available throughout.

3

Share & Log It

Everyone shows what they built. We share feedback, celebrate wins, and log it to the practice-wide Agent Tracker.
